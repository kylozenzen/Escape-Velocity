Controls & mechanics

HOW TO PLAY

📱
Tap to steer — hold left or right half of screen. Switch to Tilt in Settings.
▲
Fire button — the small pill at the bottom center. Shoots upward to destroy debris.
🟩
White platforms — solid, safe to land on.
💙
Blue platforms — moving left and right.
⬜
Cracked platforms — brittle, break on first landing.
🟡
Spring pads — yellow pad above platform, launches you extra high.
👻
Ghost platforms — fade in and out. Land while visible or fall through.
🛰️
Satellites & asteroids — shoot or dodge. Contact = fail.
⚫
Black holes — gravity pulls you in. Can't be shot. Route around them.
🌀
Wormholes — enter to teleport upward. Useful shortcut.
🛡️
Shield — cyan orb pickup. Absorbs one hazard hit. Glows around you when active.
🚀
Jetpack — orange pickup. Your next platform bounce launches extra high. One use.
❄️
Freeze — blue pickup. Freezes all hazards in place for a few seconds.
🧲
Magnet — green pickup. Expands your landing zone on platforms temporarily.
🏁
Gold checkpoint — land on it to clear the level.
